Whether it's in my coding, design outlook, or hobbies, I always strive to challenge and improve myself.

Throughout my efforts to improve my design skills, I've enjoyed reading about typography, psychology and design thinking.
Some of my favourite books include:

  • Thinking Fast and Slow - Daniel Kahneman
  • Thinking with Type - Ellen Lupton
  • Black Box Thinking - Matthew Syed
  • Emotional Design - Donald A. Norman
  • The Power of Habit - Charles Duhigg

I also enjoy learning and improving on new skills when it comes to my hobbies. Each year when I go skiing, I always try to learn a new trick and during lockdown I have also been trying to improve my skateboarding ability. I certainly have the bruises to show for it!

Whilst developing this portfolio, I chose to use Angular (despite it being over-kill for a mostly static site) in order to further improve my understanding of the framework. I became more comfortable with Redux and RxJs, using Reducers, Actions and Selectors to manage application state. I'm a huge advocate of learning-by-doing and I also learnt how to create SVG animations (visible when hovering over links on the home page), during the course of this project.
