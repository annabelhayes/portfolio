I joined DotGo (at the time called Reason8) for a 2 month contract in summer 2016, before I went to university. My role encompassed Sales, Account Management and Web Design.

I returned to DotGo in 2019 for a 3 month contract as a Content Writer (see below)

UI & UX Process

––CHALLENGE

to produce new, modern, SEO-optimised websites for existing clients that would:

  1. appeal to their users, with the capacity to convert more potential customers into actual customers,

  2. fulfill each client's own design vision and aspirations.

––RESULTS

I was proud that most clients required very little, if any, amendments. I found that dedicating a lot of time to the "Research" stage, especially analysis of competitor's websites, was crucial for success.

Return to DotGO in 2019

As the company expanded, operations were split into: Sales, Digital Design and Content Writing, with dedicated personnel for each role. I returned to DotGo in 2019 for a 3 month contract as a Content Writer.

Content Role

After reading through client’s and designer’s notes in a shared data area, I called clients’ and carried out a detailed content consultation. I then wrote up their content, aiming for 350-500 words/page and interweaving key primary and secondary SEO phrases throughout.

Examples

The Bell Inn Anslow (Pub)
TRX International (Recruitment)
Olley Waste Management
Please note, I did not design these websites, I just produced the written content. This content may have been altered slightly by clients since.

Reflections

Working at DotGo gave me with a introduction to Web Design, Marketing, SEO and Copyrighting. When selling websites, success ultimately relied on me relaying to customers why great UI & UX, alongside engaging content, is essential for every type of business. In doing so, I found that my own passion for UI & UX design grew too.

Key Learnings

  • Clear Vision - I learnt the importance of establishing a clear, shared vision with each client, in order to ensure clients were always satisfied with the end result of their websites.

  • DotGo System - I had to rapidly come to terms with the DotGo website building system, in order to build at the appropriate pace. I strongly belive that learning new things efficiently, is a skill that is enhanced through practise.

  • Marketing - Whilst Content Writing, I took on a Marketing role, writing with empathy and deploying attention-grabbing phrases to appeal to potential customers.

  • SEO - In the Frontend, with key search phrases and in the Backend, by adding meta tags to images, I became familiar with SEO practises throughout both Dotgo roles.
