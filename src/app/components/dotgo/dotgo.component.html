Return to DotGo in 2019

As the company expanded, operations were split into: Sales, Digital Design and Content Writing, with dedicated personnel for each role. I returned to DotGo in 2019 for a 3 month contract as a Content Writer, before spending a few amazing months skiing.

Role

After reading through client’s and designer’s notes in a shared data area, I called clients’ and carried out a detailed content consultation. I then wrote up their content, aiming for 350-500 words/page and interweaving key primary and secondary SEO phrases throughout.

Key Requirements
  • Create a consistent “voice” which runs throughout client's content, which fits their business and would appeal to potential customers.
  • Detailed analysis of what made each client's business unique and how to sell it.
  • Research into competitors to gain ideas about the structure and form of the content.

Examples

The Bell Inn Anslow (Pub)
Optionz (Property Investment)
Olley Waste Management
Please note, I did not design these websites, I just produced the written content. This content may have been altered slightly by clients since - so basically, if you spot anything that doesn’t sound right, please assume it was not me… ;)

Reflections

Working at DotGo gave me with great introduction to Web Design, Marketing, SEO and Copyrighting.

Key Learnings

  • Clear Vision - I learnt the importance of establishing a clear, shared vision with each client, in order to ensure clients were always satisfied with the end result of their websites.

  • DotGo System - I had to rapidly come to terms with the DotGo website building system, in order to build at the appropriate pace. I strongly belive that learning new things efficiently, is a skill that is enhanced through practise.

  • Marketing - Especially whilst Content Writing, I took on a Marketing role, writing with empathy and deploying attention-grabbing phrases to appeal to potential customers.

  • SEO - In the Frontend, with key search phrases and in the Backend, by adding meta tags to images, I became familiar with SEO practises throughout both Dotgo roles.
